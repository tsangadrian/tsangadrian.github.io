Illustrator Art

Viper, Valorant

Going into the project, I did not have much experience with figure drawing in general.

Therefore, I had trouble applying the fundamentals of drawing anatomy to the project. As

a quick solution to this issue, I used a virtual mannequin poser app called Magic Poser to

to help serve as a tool for composition, proportion, and reference. I aimed to go for a relaxed,

sitting pose for this work.

Here shows the photo of a very rough sketch of Viper on paper, referencing from the virtual mannequin.

Staying true to the original concept of the character, I used official concept art for referencing of

the details for the clothes, body size, and accessories.

The entire piece is completely vectorized on Adobe Illustrator. This image shows an xray of the anchor points.

The character's form is created by layering many color-filled, vectorized shapes above another.
