Meme Mashup

Digital Meme

Intended as a work for Art-74, this meme mashup follows an exploration into what meme culture is, and how they contribute to our modern culture. Combining two memes which are the “This is fine” meme and the “Hong Kong Protests”, I aimed to create a dialogue on the subject. Although it may seem trivializing to consider these protests as a meme, I want to mention I definitely do not intend on implying the consideration. As to what I found what a meme was to be, was an idea, concept, or behavior relayed within a culture to withhold meaning or a theme. As a personal tribute to the efforts at Hong Kong, I wanted to take a stance on the situation through representation of this meme.
