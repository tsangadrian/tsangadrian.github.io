clarity 

Digital Video

This video was created as a project for Art-75. Titled “Clarity”, this piece was created to depict the many instilled sources of gratification that digital culture has stapled onto our lifestyles. As humans, it’s sometimes hard to fight against some of the psychological mechanics that are hardwired into our brains, especially when work needs to be done. As a sentiment to this idea, I wanted to illustrate my perspective on this issue from a first-person narrative, showing a similar theme when I go about my daily life. As global work life and society transitions to a digital age, so too does accessibility and convenience. Inevitably, we need to take the adequate steps to adapt.
