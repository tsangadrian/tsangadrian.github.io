Minecraft Artwork

Companion Cube and Tyrael

The following works are some of my creations made in Minecraft. My first work is a pixel illustration inspired by the character Tyrael in Blizzard Entertainment’s franchise Diablo. Spanning well over a thousand blocks, it was especially hard finding the correct color scheme using a limited amount of colored blocks offered at the time of building, so I opted to try my best to interpret the best fit for each area of the sculpture. The second work was created during my time in Art-74. This 3D sculpture of Valve’s Companion Cube from the Portal franchise was at the time what I thought was a perfect fit on exploring ideas of converting virtual creations to tangible 3D objects. The Companion Cube’s story is canonically based around the idea of accompanying you throughout your adventure, and specifically to assist you within the confines of the facility in the game. However, I felt like creating a 3D modeled sculpture of it pays proper tribute to what it's supposed to represent—of resilience, cooperation, and compassion, while breaking the third wall and as an act of defiance by bringing it to the real world with you.
