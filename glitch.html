Maplestory

This series aims to explore the aesthetic possibilities of glitch art, created during Art-74. The content of the three images are glitched images of various monsters from the game Maplestory. This game will always hold a place in my memories, usually evoking some nostalgic feelings. Because of this, I was inspired to capture that nostalgia through a representation of glitching. My memories canâ€™t be completely crystal-clear with recalling my experiences when I was a kid, so the distortion acts as this inherent haziness. However, something that is inexplicably clear that I remember, are its charming visuals and whimsical culture peering through the cracks of my memories. These three pieces attempt to embody this idea.
