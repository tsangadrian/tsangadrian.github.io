Spiral 

Digital Sculpture

During my studies in Art 2, our class was tasked to explore the concept of the spiral/labyrinth. This series of work is based off of these conceptual topics. Titled 'shroud', 'harpoon', and 'destruction' chronologically, I wanted each piece to be interconnected in terms of theme, while also paying homage to the initial concept. The interconnected themes related through all three pieces are that of environmental hazards and climate change. Continuing into my exploration into Unity, these works are some of my few early projects getting to learn the mechanics of the engine. They are all simple digital sculptural installations within the Unity space incorporating a minimalist aesthetic.My hopes are to bring awareness to the consequent hazards of specific human activity on ecosystems.
